The First Haitian Wesleyan Church has been in the Triad area for 14 years helping with the spiritual and social needs of the community. If you are new, visiting or a long time resident of the Triad area and searching for a place to worship, we extend an invitation to you. We are a small Christian Church in High Point, we welcome everyone regardless of denomination.
